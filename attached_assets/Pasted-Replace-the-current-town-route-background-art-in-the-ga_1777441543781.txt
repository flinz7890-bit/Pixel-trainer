scene with an <img> tag pointing to LOCATION_IMAGES[currentZone.name].
Style the image to fill the scene container (width: 100%, height: 100%,
object-fit: cover, imageRendering: 'pixelated').

If any image fails to load (onerror event), silently fall back to the
existing CSS-generated scene for that zone. Never show a broken image
icon. Do not break any other existing functionality.
